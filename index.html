Insecurity

Definition on Self-Esteem and Insecure

Self-Esteem meaning being confidence in one's own worth or abilities; self-respect.

Insecure means that (of a person) not confident or assured; uncertain and anxious.

What being Insecure does to your Mental Health

Insecurity and mental health problems such as depression and anxiety have a reciprocal relationship. Insecurity is also linked to personality disorders such as narcissistic personality disorder and borderline personality disorder.

Some ways you can get support and how you can support someone.

Some way you can get help is by talking to a therapist or someone in your life about how you're feeling. You can also focus on yourself and not others around you. Some ways you can support someone if they're insecure is by just listening to what they have to say, Encourage them, express your care and concerns.
